~ Summary ~

Join Running Guy in his first ever adventure through the Pink Cloud Kingdom! Normally Running Guy just runs around his problems but here in Cloud Kingdom there is no floor he can run on! Luckly a wizard gives Running Guy a potion allowing him to fly by flapping his legs. Running Guy needs your help as he flies through the vast city in the clouds. Use Running Guy's new power to fly all over Cloud Kingdom but don't get too careless Running Guy is flying too fast to run into any city buildings. That is where you come in, help Running Guy fly safely through Cloud Kingdom!
